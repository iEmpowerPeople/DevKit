## DevKit sync maintenance

Refer to `scripts/README.md` when you change any of the following:

- `library/` or `profiles/` contents, update-profile rules, or catalogue generation.
- `scripts/sync-adapter.py`, `scripts/update-profile.py`, or `scripts/gen-catalogue.sh` behavior or flags.
- Tool checks or installs in `scripts/check-tools.*` or `scripts/install-required.*`.
